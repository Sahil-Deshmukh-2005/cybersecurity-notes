# Network Categories

There are many netwrok categories but we are only gonna cover 2 of them.  

***

## LAN (Local Area Network) 
It has very short range.  
ex., small bussinesses, building, house, etc...

1) Client/Server LAN :-
A central server is connected to all the other devices or clients.

2) Peer-to-Peer LAN :-
Client or devices are connected to each other directly.

***

## WAN (Wide Area Network)
Its a Collection of multiple LANs.  
ex., Different regions, globally, etc...

1) Public WAN :-
A public infrastructure that uses public services for communication, such as the `Internet`.
2) Private WAN :-
Uses dedicated connections between geographically dispersed sites.
