## Room-3 : Careers in Cyber

### Security Analysis:
Security ananlysts are often referred to as the digital defenders of an organization and sit on the `blue team`.  
Using their skills, they investigate and piece together potential security incidents.

#### A day as a Security Analysts involves:
```
*️Monitering activity taking place on the devices and network of the organization.
*️Investigating unusual or suspicious activity, such as strange logins.
*️Piecing together information to understand what has happened , when and how.
*️Working with other teams to improve the organization's defence. 
```

***

### Security Engineering:
Security Engineers build and maintain the system and processes that protect an organization's network and devices and are known as the architects or cyber security.  
A Security Engineer will be responsible for maintaining an `Intrusion Detection System (IDS)`.  

#### A day as a Security Engineers involves:
```
*️Designinh and maintaining security systems.
*️Keeping up with the latest and greatest hacker techniques being used.
*️Dcoumenting processes and procedures.
*️Assessing risks and making sure systems and applications are protected against vulnerabilities.
```

***

### Penetration Testing:
A peneration Tester's job is to check how secure a company's systems and software are by safely trying to break into them.  
They search for weaknesses and vulnerabilities and then test if they can be exploited.  
Everything is done under an agreement between the company and the tester. This process is called `engagement`.

#### A day as a Penetration Testers involves:
```
*️Testing the security of computer systems, networks and websites.
*️Performing security assessments, audits, and analysing security policies.
*️Analyzing the results and creating repoerts, and advising an organization on how to prevent the attack from occuring.
```
