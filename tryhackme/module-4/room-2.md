# Module-4 : Computer Fundamentals 

## Room-2 : Computer Types

***

Objective of this room is to identify and distinguish between different types of computers you use  
directly, such as laptops and smartphones,   
and indirectly, such as servers, IoT Devices, and embedded systems.   

***

1) Laptop :  
   Portable everyday computing.  
2) Desktop :  
   Sustained performance at fixed location.
3) Workstation :  
   Precision and reliability for professional tasks.
4) Servers :  
   Providing Services to many users over a network with no screen or keyboard.
5) Smartphones :  
   Pocket-size computers optimized for battery life and connectivity.  
6) Tablet :  
   Touch-First computer with larger screen.  
7) Iot Devices :  
   Network connected devices with single purpose.
8) Embedded computers :   
   Computers built into another devices.
