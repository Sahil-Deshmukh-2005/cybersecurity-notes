### Logging and Authentication

Windows commonly uses account types:  
1) Guest = A restricted account intented for temporary access, with minimal permissions and no ability to change system settings.
2) Standard = A user account for everyday tasks, without access to system-wide changes.
3) Administrator = A priviledged account with full control over the system, including software installation, conifguration changes, and user management.
