# Module-7 : Attacks and Defences

## Room-1 : The CIA Triad

***

What does Cyber Security actually protect?  
It Protects 3 key aspects:  

***

### C : Confidentiality
Meaning,  
Digital information can only be accessed by authorized person only.  

### I : Integrity
Meaning,  
Digital information can not be modified without permissions and can only be changed by authorized person.  

### A : Availability 
Meaning,  
Digital infromation is always available to access to its users.  

***

#### When any Cyber incident occurs, Security professionals generally start by asking:  
1) Was sensitive data exposed to unauthorized individuals.
2) Was data being modified without permissions.
3) Were systems and services unavailable to users when they needed.
